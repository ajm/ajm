Alan Medlar

I am a research scientist at the University of Helsinki, where I co-lead the Exploratory Search and Personalization research group with Dorota Glowacka.

I build interactive systems to help users navigate complex data sets and am interested in user behaviour and experience as it relates to search.

In a previous life, I worked on genomics and genetic epidemiology.
